RESULT DISCRIPTION
    Appendix II: USAID ECAP I Result Framework
    Goal: To mitigate the impact of HIV and improve the health and well-being of Vulnerable Children and Adolescents (VCA) through the delivery of high-impact, evidence-based, and age-appropriate interventions with a family-centered, customized approach for each VCA sub-population
    Objective 1. Support VCAs and their families to be healthy by improving access to health/HIV services
    IR1.1: Increased diagnosis of HIV infection
    1.1.1 Strengthened mother-baby pair tracking of HEI
    1.1.2 Enhanced index family tracing
    1.1.3 Increased completed referrals for HIV testing
    1.1.4 Increased disclosure support and stigma reduction
    IR1.2: Increased HIV treatment, adherence, retention, and viral suppression
    1.2.1 Increased linkage of C/ALHIV to treatment
    1.2.2 Enhanced delivery of family-centered, differentiated services for treatment support
    1.2.3 Increased community-based adherence support
    1.2.4 Increased viral load suppression
    1.2.5 Strengthened linkage between community-based OVC activities and facility-based clinical activities to ensure access to the full cascade of services
    Objective 3. Support VCAs and their families to be safe by improving child protection and prevention of SGBV
    IR3.1: Reduced risk of physical, emotional, and psychological injury due to violence
    3.1.1 Strengthen family cohesion and positive parenting skills of caregivers
    3.1.2 Increased psychosocial and mental health support
    3.1.3 Increased participation of community leaders and members in the prevention of SGBV and linkages to post-SGBV services
    3.1.4 Increased primary prevention of HIV and violence skills of VCA
    Objective 4. Support VCAs and their families to be schooled by improving school retention and progression
    IR4.1: Increased school retention and promotion
    4.1.1 Increased access to primary, secondary, and vocational education
    4.1.2 Increased education support
    4.1.3 Increased progression monitoring and support
    4.1.4 Enhanced abilities of teachers and support staff to support VCA
    Cross-Cutting Thematic Area: Gender
    Provision of gender-appropriate and gender-inclusive support to VCA and their families, understanding and addressing the unique cultural barriers opposed to gender norms in Zambia
